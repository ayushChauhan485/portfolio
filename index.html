My Story

As a third-year Civil Engineering student at Punjab Engineering College, I contribute as Secretary of the Projection and Design Club, where I lead a group of around 100 students to foster a community focused on design and video editing. My role allows me to inspire collaboration and share essential skills in scripting, shooting, and editing in today’s digital landscape. At the American Society of Civil Engineers, I lead the Data and Logistics team, focusing on data analysis and logistical efficiency for events. As a Research Intern at CSIR India, I collaborated on projects addressing rural accessibility and transport network challenges using methodologies like MCDM and machine learning. Proficient in QGIS, Quantum GIS, and statistical data analysis, I aim to bridge technical knowledge with creative problem-solving.

Alongside these roles, my academic work is aligned with SDGs-2030 and focuses on sustainable urban systems, AI-driven transport planning, environmental data modeling, and spatial analytics through peer-reviewed publications and conference submissions.
